International activity

Senators took part in the 23rd meeting of the Commission on Cooperation between the Senate of Parliament and the Federation Council

21.04.2025 58

The 23rd meeting of the Commission on Cooperation between the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan and the Federation Council of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation was dedicated to strengthening interregional ties and developing bilateral initiatives in strategic areas. Led by Deputy Chairman of the Senate Zhakip Asanov, the event was also attended by senators Marat Kozhayev, Shakarym Buktugutov, Zhanna Asanova, Olga Bulavkina and Nuriya Niyazova.

Senate Speaker Addresses Ceremonial Session of CIS IPA

18.04.2025 87

The 58th plenary session of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the CIS Member Nations is currently taking place in St. Petersburg with the participation of the Heads of the Chambers of Parliament of the Commonwealth countries. The Kazakh delegation was headed by the Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev. As part of the visit, he spoke at the ceremonial session of the CIS IPA dedicated to the 80th anniversary of the Great Victory and held a number of bilateral meetings.

Amangeldy Nugmanov took part in the meeting of TurkPA

16.04.2025 196

Senator Amangeldy Nugmanov took part in the second meeting of the Chairmen of the Defense and Security Committees of the Parliaments of the member countries of the Parliamentary Assembly of Turkic States. The event was dedicated to the topic "Strengthening Security and Defense Cooperation between the Turkic States: The Role of Parliaments".

Senate Speaker Meets with Saudi Arabian Advisory Council Chairman

14.04.2025 363

Speaker of the Senate of the Parliament, Head of the Secretariat of the Congress of Leaders of World and Traditional Religions Maulen Ashimbayev arrived in Saudi Arabia at the invitation of the Consultative Council. In Riyadh, a meeting was held with the Chairman of the Consultative Council of the Kingdom Abdullah bin Mohammed bin Ibrahim Al Ash-Sheikh and the Minister of Islamic Affairs, Call and Guidance Abdulatif bin Abdulaziz bin Abdrakhman Al Ash-Sheikh. The parties discussed the prospects for strengthening inter-parliamentary cooperation and spiritual diplomacy, and outlined the tasks of joint work for the coming period.

Olga Perepechina discussed legal reforms in Kazakhstan at the session of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe

11.04.2025 182

Deputy Chairperson of the Senate Olga Perepechina took part in a round table meeting within the framework of the session of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe on the topic "Democratic reforms - the basis of stability and prosperity" in the city of Strasbourg. The Chairperson of the Constitutional Court of the Republic of Kazakhstan Elvira Azimova made a report during the meeting.

Senate Speaker Meets with Sierra Leone Foreign Minister

08.04.2025 200

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the Republic of Sierra Leone Timothy Musa Kabbah.

Strengthening interparliamentary cooperation between Kazakhstan and Israel discussed in the Senate

08.04.2025 213

Senate Chairman Maulen Ashimbayev held talks with Speaker of the Knesset of the State of Israel Amir Ohana. The parties outlined prospects for further strengthening inter-parliamentary cooperation between the two countries.

Vice-Speaker of the Senate Zhakip Asanov took part in the work of the 150th Assembly of the Inter-Parliamentary Union

07.04.2025 186

Deputy Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Zhakip Asanov took part in the work of the 150th Assembly of the Inter-Parliamentary Union. Together with parliamentarians from other countries, the Vice-Speaker took part in the general debate on the topic "Parliamentary activity in the interests of social development and justice".

Human Rights Protection: Aigul Kapbarova took part in the work of the Permanent Commission of the IPA CIS

02.04.2025 398

Today, a meeting of the Standing Commission of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly on Social Policy and Human Rights was held in St. Petersburg. Deputies of the Parliaments of the CIS member states took part in its work. Kazakhstan was represented online by member of the Senate Committee on Social and Cultural Development and Science, Senator Aigul Kapbarova.

Senate Speaker meets with Slovenian President

31.03.2025 261

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev met with the President of Slovenia Natasa Pirc-Musar, who arrived in Kazakhstan on an official visit. During the meeting, the parties discussed issues of cooperation between the two countries and exchanged proposals to strengthen interparliamentary cooperation.

Barrier to Domestic Violence: Initiative of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Receives Support at the CIS Level

28.03.2025 215

Today in St. Petersburg, at the second meeting of the Expert Council under the Permanent Commission of the IPA CIS on Legal Issues, a model draft law "On the legal regulation of combating domestic violence" developed on the initiative of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan, revised taking into account the proposals of experts, was considered. The document was prepared taking into account the legislative experience of Kazakhstan and the CIS countries. Its goal is to protect citizens from violence, prevent it and form effective legal mechanisms.

Senate Speaker Meets with US House of Representatives Member

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Maulen Ashimbayev held a meeting with member of the House of Representatives of the US Congress Carol Miller

The Senate hosted a meeting of the CIS IPA Permanent Commission on Agrarian Policy, Natural Resources and Ecology

13.03.2025 432

A meeting of the Standing Commission of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly on Agrarian Policy, Natural Resources and Ecology was held within the walls of the Senate of the Parliament of Kazakhstan. The meeting was attended by Deputy Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Zhakip Asanov, Chairman of the Standing Commission of the CIS IPA on Agrarian Policy, Natural Resources and Ecology - Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Olga Bulavkina, parliamentarians of the CIS IPA member states, representatives of relevant Ministries of Kazakhstan, experts in the field of agro-industrial complex and ecology.

A meeting of the Control and Budget Commission of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly was held in the Tauride Palace in St. Petersburg, in which Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Sergey Karplyuk took part.
