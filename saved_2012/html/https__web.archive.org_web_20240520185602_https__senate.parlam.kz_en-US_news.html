The law on digitalization and improvement of the investment climate was approved by senators

02.05.2024 263

A meeting of the Chamber was held under the chairmanship of the Speaker of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Maulen Ashimbayev, at which deputies considered a number of laws and voiced their parliamentary requests.

Laws on strengthening social protection of scientists were presented in the Senate

30.04.2024 242

The deputies of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan were presented with the laws "On Science and Technology Policy", "On amendments and additions to some legislative acts of the Republic of Kazakhstan on science and technology policy, platform employment and state control".

Senators discussed the issues of improving human rights mechanisms

30.04.2024 245

Deputy Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Zhakip Asanov and deputies of the Committee on Constitutional Legislation, Judicial System and Law Enforcement Agencies met with judges of the Constitutional Court. The participants of the meeting discussed issues of further strengthening the activities of the Constitutional Court and interaction in matters of current legal monitoring of legislation, as well as effective rule-making activities. 

Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Speaker of the Parliament of Singapore

30.04.2024 241

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of Kazakhstan Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Speaker of the Parliament of Singapore, Cea Kian Peng, who arrived on an official visit to the Republic of Kazakhstan. During the meeting, the parties discussed the prospects for investment cooperation and further strengthening of inter-parliamentary cooperation.

The Senate is considering the issues of improving the Auyl Amanaty project

30.04.2024 242

Deputies of the Senate Committee on Agrarian Issues, Environmental Management and Rural Development held a meeting with the leadership of the Ministry of Agriculture and the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection of the Population on the implementation of the Auyl Amanaty project.

Measures to combat illegal withdrawal of capital abroad discussed in the Senate

30.04.2024 32

Deputies of the Senate Finance and Budget Committee held a round table meeting on the topic "Implementation of currency regulation in order to prevent illegal withdrawal of capital."

The new senator will be sworn in at the upcoming Senate meeting

29.04.2024 39

The Speaker of the Senate, Maulen Ashimbayev, held a meeting of the Bureau, during which the deputies determined the agenda of the meeting of the Chamber scheduled for May 2.

The role of business in the implementation of the SDG agenda was discussed in the Senate

29.04.2024 162

Under the chairmanship of Deputy Chairman of the Senate Olga Perepechina, a working meeting was held in preparation for the meeting of the Parliamentary Commission on Monitoring the implementation of national goals and objectives in the field of sustainable development on the topic "ESG transformation: the role of business in the implementation of the Sustainable Development Agenda".

Olga Bulavkina discussed issues of compensation for damage to the population after a man-made accident at the Ridder cascade hydroelectric power plant

29.04.2024 34
Olga Bulavkina, a member of the Senate Committee on Agrarian Issues, Environmental Management and Rural Development, held a working meeting with interested government agencies, where they discussed issues of compensation for damage to the population as a result of a man-made emergency at the Ridder Cascade hydroelectric power plant in 2023.

The presentation of the law "On Mass Media" took place in the Senate

29.04.2024 41

The deputies of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan were presented with the laws "On Mass Media", "On amendments and additions to some legislative acts of the Republic of Kazakhstan on mass media issues", "On Amendments to the Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan on Administrative Offenses on Mass Media issues".

Senators discussed the issues of transformation of the educational system and protection of children's rights

27.04.2024 32

Deputies of the Senate Committee on Socio-Cultural Development and Science held a visiting meeting in Kyzylorda on the topic "Transformation of the educational system and priority aspects of protecting children's rights." The meeting participants discussed issues of further improvement of the national education system, access and quality to preschool, secondary, technical and vocational education, as well as problems of ensuring the protection of children's rights and inclusive education.

Prospects for improving the work of customs services

27.04.2024 34

Deputies of the Senate Finance and Budget Committee visited the Zhibek Zholy checkpoint, where they met with representatives of the Committee and the State Revenue Department of the Ministry of Finance, the Border Service and the business community. The meeting participants discussed the activities of the customs post in the field of public services through an information system, electronic support, as well as improving customs administration.

Senators took part in the environmental campaign "Taza Kazakhstan"

27.04.2024 30

Deputies of the Senate of the Parliament and staff members took part in the republican environmental action "Taza Kazakhstan", initiated by the President of the country Kassym-Jomart Tokayev. During the citywide clean-up, about 100 seedlings of ash, pine and birch trees were planted.

Senator Asem Rakhmetova took part in the International Forum on Sustainable Development

26.04.2024 29

Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament Asem Rakhmetova, as part of the delegation from Kazakhstan, took part in the International Forum on the topic "Trans-Altai Dialogue on Sustainable Development. Peace, justice and effective institutions", which took place in the city of Ulaanbaatar.

Senators proposed measures to develop the exploration industry

26.04.2024 35

Deputies of the Senate Committee on Economic Policy, Innovative Development and Entrepreneurship held a round table meeting on the topic "Topical issues of exploration development in the Republic of Kazakhstan as part of the implementation of the President's Address to the People of Kazakhstan "The Economic Course of a Fair Kazakhstan".
