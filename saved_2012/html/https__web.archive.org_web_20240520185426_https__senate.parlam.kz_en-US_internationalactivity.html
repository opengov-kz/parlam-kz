International activity

The Speaker of the Senate met with the Hero of the Kyrgyz Republic Medetkan Sherimkulov

19.04.2024 264

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev held a meeting with former Chairman of the Supreme Council of Kyrgyzstan, Hero of the Kyrgyz Republic, member of the Council of Elders of the Organization of Turkic States Medetkan Sherimkulov. The interlocutors discussed the role of the intelligentsia of the two countries in further strengthening cultural and humanitarian cooperation, as well as in the framework of interaction on international platforms.

Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Director of the Oxford Center for Islamic Studies

17.04.2024 205

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev held a meeting with Director of the Oxford Center for Islamic Studies Farhan Ahmad Nizami, during which prospects for strengthening research ties and cooperation in the field of spiritual diplomacy were discussed.

Ainur Argynbekova, as part of the OSCE PA delegation, took part in the discussion of climate change issues

12.04.2024 144

Member of the Senate of the Parliament Ainur Argynbekova, as part of the delegation of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe, headed by the Organization's Special Representative for Central Asia Farah Karimi, discussed with experts the prospects for further strengthening interparliamentary cooperation in various fields.

The Speaker of the Senate met with the Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia

12.04.2024 101

Senate Speaker Maulen Ashimbayev met with Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia Shalva Papuashvili, who arrived on his first official visit to the Republic of Kazakhstan. The parties discussed a number of topical issues, including prospects for further strengthening interparliamentary cooperation.

The Speaker of the Senate met with the delegation of the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly

09.04.2024 384

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev met with a delegation of the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly headed by the Special Representative for Central Asia Farah Karimi. The parties discussed the prospects for further development of cooperation, including within the framework of international organizations.

Sergey Karplyuk took part in the meeting of the IPA CIS Commission

04.04.2024 355

Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Sergey Karplyuk took part in a meeting of the Control and Budget Commission of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the CIS.

The Speaker of the Senate met with the Pope and took part in an interfaith conference

04.04.2024 147

Chairman of the Senate Maulen Ashimbayev and a group of deputies paid a working visit to the Vatican and the Italian Republic. During the visit, an audience with Pope Francis was held, as well as a round table meeting organized by the Dicastery for Interreligious Dialogue of the Vatican and the Secretariat of the Congress of Leaders of World and Traditional Religions. In addition, the senators discussed prospects for strengthening interparliamentary cooperation with deputies of the Senate of the Parliament of the Italian Republic.

Galiaskar Sarybayev took part in the meetings of the IPA CIS commissions

28.03.2024 213

Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Galiaskar Sarybayev took part in the work of the commissions of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the CIS.

Deputies of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan met with deputies of the Federation Council of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation

19.03.2024 280

Deputy Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Asanov Zhakip and Senator Sergey Ershov met with First Deputy Chairman of the Federation Council of the Federal Assembly of Russia Andrey Yatskin and Deputy Chairman of the Federation Council of the Federal Assembly Konstantin Kosachev.

Ruslan Rustemov took part as an observer of the CSTO PA for the presidential elections in Russia

19.03.2024 365

Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Ruslan Rustemov took part as an observer of the presidential elections in the Russian Federation from the CSTO Parliamentary Assembly.

Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Ambassador of Mongolia to Kazakhstan

05.03.2024 280

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Ambassador of Mongolia to Kazakhstan Dorj Bayarkhuu. The interlocutors discussed issues of further strengthening interparliamentary cooperation and expanding partnership between the two countries.

Ainur Argynbekova took part in the 23rd meeting of the OSCE PA

23.02.2024 480

Deputy of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Ainur Argynbekova took part in the 23rd winter session of the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly.

The Speaker of the Senate met with the Turkish Ambassador to Kazakhstan

21.02.2024 498

Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Maulen Ashimbayev held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Turkey to Kazakhstan Mustafa Kapudzhu. The parties discussed issues of further strengthening cooperation and expanding the strategic partnership between the two countries.

Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Apostolic Nuncio of the Holy See in Kazakhstan

14.02.2024 600
Chairman of the Senate of the Parliament Maulen Ashimbayev met with the Apostolic Nuncio of the Holy See in the Republic of Kazakhstan, George George Panamtundil. The interlocutors discussed issues of strengthening cooperation between the two countries and further expanding partnership in promoting interreligious dialogue.

Vice-Speaker of the Senate Zhakip Asanov met with the Chairman of the Milli Majlis of Azerbaijan

09.02.2024 386

Observers from the Interparliamentary Assembly of the CIS Member States, headed by Coordinator Vice Speaker of the Senate of the Parliament of the Republic of Kazakhstan Zhakip Asanov, met with Chairman of the Milli Majlis of Azerbaijan Sagiba Gafarova.
